Chicken Tikka Masala

Description:

Chicken tikka masala is a popular Indian dish that consists of small pieces of marinated chicken (called "tikka") that are grilled or roasted and then simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce (called "masala"). The chicken is first marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala, which gives it a flavorful and slightly tangy taste.

Ingredients:

  • 450g boneless, skinless chicken thigh, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, tumeric, paprika, salt, cayenne pepper, ginger, and garlic. Stir until well combined.

  2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the Marinade. Cover the bowl with cling wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours.

  3. Preheat the over to 200C. Line a baking tray with parchment paper or foil.

  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, leaving a little space between each piece. Brush the chicken with the vegetable oil.

  5. Place the skewers on the prepared baking tray and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and lightly browned.

  6. Serve with a side of naan bread or rice. Enjoy!
