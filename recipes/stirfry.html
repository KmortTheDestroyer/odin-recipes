Chicken Stirfry

Description:

Chicken stir-fry is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that is perfect for a quick weeknight meal. This dish typically involves stir-frying thinly sliced chicken with a variety of colorful vegetables and aromatic spices in a wok or large frying pan. The result is a flavorful and nutritious meal that can be served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red capsicum, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 green capsicum, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 onion, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the sliced chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch. Toss to coat and let marinate for 15 minutes.

  2. Heat a wok over high heat. Add vegetable oil and swirl to coat the wok.

  3. Add the chicken and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes or until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the wok and set aside.

  4. Add the green and red capsicum, onion, garlic, and ginger to the same wok and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Return the chicken to the wok and stir-fry everything for another 1-2 minutes.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve with rice or noodles. Enjoy :)
