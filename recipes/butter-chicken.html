Butter Chicken

Description:

The beautiful Butter Chicken Sauce mostly gets its richness from cream. While some restaurants take it over the top by stirring in a (very!) generous amount of butter into the sauce at the end, I find that it’s rich enough as it is. The Butter Chicken flavour and experience – but less oily than restaurant versions!

Ingredients:

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ginger, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 750g chicken thigh fillet, cut into bite size pieces

Curry

  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 1 cup tomato passata
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 tbs salt

Steps:

  1. Marinade: Combine the Marinade ingredients with the chicken in a bowl Cover and refrigerate overnight, or up to 24 hours.

  2. Cook chicken: Heat the ghee over high heat in a large frying pan. Take the chicken out of the Marinade but do not wipe or shake off the Marinade from the chicken.

  3. Place chicken in frying pan and cook for about 3 minutes, or until the chicken is white all over.

  4. Sauce: Add the tomato passata, cream, sugar and salt. Also add any remaining Marinade left to the bowl. Turn down to low and simmer for 20 minutes.

  5. Garnish with coriander leaves if using. Serve with basmati rice.
