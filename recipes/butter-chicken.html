Butter Chicken

Description:

The beautiful Butter Chicken Sauce mostly gets its richness from cream. While some restaurants take it over the top by stirring in a (very!) generous amount of butter into the sauce at the end, I find that it’s rich enough as it is. The Butter Chicken flavour and experience – but less oily than restaurant versions!

Ingredients:

Marinade

    1/2 cup plain yogurt 1 tbsp lemon juice 1 tsp tumeric powder 2 tsp garam masala 1/2 tsp chilli powder 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tbsp ginger, freshly grated 2 cloves garlic, crushed 750g chicken thigh fillet, cut into bite size pieces

Curry

    2 tbsp ghee or butter 1 cup tomato passata 1 cup heavy cream 1 tbsp sugar 1 1/4 tbs salt

Steps:

    Marinade: Combine the Marinade ingredients with the chicken in a bowl Cover and refrigerate overnight, or up to 24 hours.
